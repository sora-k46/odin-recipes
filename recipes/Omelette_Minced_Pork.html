Omelette with Minced Pork

Not a normal Omelette!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Minced Pork
  • Soy sauce
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs and add soy sauce
  2. Add Minced Pork to the pan
  3. Add the beated eggs to the pan
  4. Turn off the gas and add pepper
