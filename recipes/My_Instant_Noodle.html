Mama Instant Noodle

Instant Noodle with Pork and eggs

Easy and cheap but taste good

Ingredients

  • Minced Pork
  • Eggs
  • Lime
  • Chili
  • Mama Instant Noodle

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add Minced Pork
  3. Add the noodle
  4. Turn off the gas and add lime and chili
